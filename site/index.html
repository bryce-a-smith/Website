About Me

I am an engineer with a background in production operations, platform delivery, and software development -- built across two years in enterprise financial services at BNY Mellon. I'm drawn to systems work: understanding how things connect, what breaks when something fails, and how to build toward resilience. I do my best thinking when I can see the full picture.

My experience spans both sides of the engineering-business divide -- monitoring 100+ production environments in a 24/7 operations setting, automating incident workflows, contributing to platform migrations, and owning cross-functional delivery on an enterprise CRM platform. I approach problems the same way regardless of which side I'm on: investigate the root cause, understand the system, fix the right thing.

I'm currently deepening my technical foundation through an accelerated B.S./M.S. in Computer Science at WGU. The projects on this site are part of that same practice -- building real infrastructure, breaking it, documenting what happened, and building it better.
